⚠️ AI Security Challenges

Modern AI systems introduce unique risks including Prompt Injection, Model Poisoning, Data Leakage, and Governance Challenges. Organizations must adopt security-by-design principles to secure AI throughout its lifecycle.

🏗️ Security by Design

Security should be integrated from the architecture phase rather than treated as a final checklist activity. Key focus areas include:

  • Threat Modeling
  • Secure Architecture Reviews
  • Cloud Security Assessments
  • Continuous Security Testing
  • Governance & Compliance

🚀 Future of Cybersecurity
